Game Instructions

🎯 Game Objective

The AI secretly selects an animal. Your goal is to guess the hidden animal by asking intelligent YES/NO questions.

❓ Question Rules

  • Questions must be YES/NO type questions.
  • Questions should contain proper grammar.
  • Questions should contain proper spelling.
  • The AI replies only with: Yes , No , or Invalid Question .
  • Every valid AI response consumes a very small AI-processing cost (approximately 0.1 Indian paise per question).
  • Maximum question length is 15 words.

💡 Example Questions

✅ Valid Questions

  • Is it a mammal?
  • Can it fly?
  • Does it live in water?
  • Is it a carnivorous animal?

❌ Invalid Questions

  • Tell me about tiger
  • What animal are you?
  • Animal?
  • Mammal or not

🐾 Guessing Rules

  • If you think you know the animal, enter its name in the animal-guess input box.
  • The spelling of the guessed animal must be correct.
  • Every wrong animal guess reduces your final score.

⏳ Question Limit

  • You can ask a maximum of 20 questions.
  • If you cannot guess the animal within 20 questions, you lose the game.

🎮 Difficulty Levels

Level Level Factor (L)
Beginner 0.8
Easy 0.9
Medium 1.0
Hard 1.11
Expert 1.25

📊 Scoring Formula

Score = 100 × (1 - q 25L ) × e-(10c + 0.3w)
  • q = Number of questions asked
  • c = Total AI cost in Indian rupees
  • w = Number of wrong animal guesses
  • L = Level factor

🏆 Scoring Meaning

  • Fewer questions → Higher score
  • Lower AI cost → Higher score
  • Fewer wrong guesses → Higher score
  • Higher difficulty levels → Higher score
  • Using all 20 questions → Score becomes 0
  • Efficient gameplay is rewarded heavily
